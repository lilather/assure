Our focus on responsive design

Responsive design is a way to create websites so that the content adjusts to match the screen size on which it is viewed.

The goal is to create a great user experience, no matter if a user is viewing your website on their desktop, tablet, or mobile phone.

The reason responsive design is so vital is that everyone has a mobile device, mobile internet has grown 504% in daily media consumption since 2011.

If your website is not responsive you are losing customers and hurting your business.

The benefits of responsive design are more customers and an increase in revenue for your business.

Our Focus on Page Speed

Page speed is a measure of how fast the content on a web page loads. Page speed is usually determined in two ways.

page load time" (the time it takes to fully display the content on a specific page) or "time to first byte" (how long it takes for your browser to receive the first byte of information from the webserver).

When your website is slow it also affects your SEO, page speed is a direct ranking factor.

Google might rank your web page lower in the SERPs if the page takes a long time to load.

A slow website also affects the user experience, studies show how users expect pages to load within two seconds, and after three seconds, up to 40% of users will abandon your site.

A web page that loads under three seconds should be your target.

To test your individual pages for page speed there are various tools,

Including PageSpeed Insights and GT Metrix

Our Focus on Accessibility

Accessibility is the practice of making your websites usable by as many people as possible, this mainly refers to people with disabilities that cannot navigate a standard website with a mouse or keyboard.

There should be no barriers that prevent people who experience varying degrees of visual, auditory, cognitive, speech, and physical disabilities to access and interact with your website.

Most Fort Worth-based business starts locally and want to grow globally, but before you make your company global make sure your website is set up to handle the millions of disabled people that need your website to be accessible to them.

The benefits of accessibility are building a broader customer base, and increasing brand loyalty through inclusion.
