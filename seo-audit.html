An audit focuses on three types of SEO.

  1. On-page SEO refers to optimizing the content of your website.
  2. Off-page SEO refers to potential actions or techniques that are taken outside of your website.
  3. Technical SEO refers to the optimization of your website to make it easy for a search engine to crawl and index your site.
